# 🐼 Everlook Guild

![Everlook Logo](/everlook_logo.png)

Welcome to the official recruitment page for the **Everlook**! We are a prominent guild on Gilly-SMP looking for more players to join our ranks. 

## 📖 About Us

The Everlook are a group of dedicated players who thrive in the challenges of the server. Whether you are an experienced builder, a fearsome PvP warrior, or a master of the server's economy, there is a place for you in our halls.

We believe in:
- **Camaraderie**: Working together to achieve common goals.
- **Our Challenge**: Being able to provide you anything you may wish.
## ⚔️ What We Offer

- **A Safe Haven**: Secure claims where you can build and store your loot safely.
- **Team Events**: Organized exploration of the Nether, the End, and team-based activities.
- **Resource Sharing and Gathering**: Access to a pool of resources, gathered by all of us.
- **Guidance**: Help for newer players navigating the server's features such as Lifesteal and custom enchants.

## 📝 How to Join

We are currently looking for active players who are respectful and ready to contribute. 

To join the Everlook:
1. Join the Gilly-SMP server (see the [How to Join](/how-to-join) page if you haven't already).
2. Reach out to RadisChat in-game or on discord.

*Everlook is waiting for you.*
