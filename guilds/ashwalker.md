# 🌋 Ashwalkers Guild

![Ashwalkers Logo](/ashwalkers_logo.png)

Welcome to the official recruitment page for the **Ashwalkers**! We are a prominent guild on Gilly-SMP looking for more players to join our ranks. 

## 📖 About Us

The Ashwalkers are a group of dedicated players who thrive in the challenges of the server. Whether you are an experienced builder, a fearsome PvP warrior, or a master of the server's economy, there is a place for you in our halls.

We believe in:
- **Camaraderie**: Working together to achieve common goals.
- **Strength**: Defending our claims and our fellow members.
- **Progression**: Maximizing our skills and building magnificent bases.

## ⚔️ What We Offer

- **A Safe Haven**: Secure claims where you can build and store your loot safely.
- **Resource Sharing**: Access to our guild's community resources.

## 📝 How to Join

We are currently looking for active players who are respectful and ready to contribute. 

To join the Ashwalkers:
1. Join the Gilly-SMP server (see the [How to Join](/how-to-join) page if you haven't already).
2. Reach out to one Geckzird in-game.
3. Show your dedication to the server and your willingness to be a team player!

*Rise from the ashes. Walk with us.*
