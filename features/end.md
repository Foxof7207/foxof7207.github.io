# The END

Fight the Ender Dragon with special mechanics and challenges!

---

### What's different ?

- **Dimension lock**: The END will be closed until a certain progression has been reached. 

- **Cooperative Fight**: Each player contributes up to 3 Eyes of Ender. All contributors must be present to activate the portal.

- **Harder Dragon**: The Ender Dragon has **1000 HP** and gains resistance as it takes damage.

- **Dragon Egg Buffs**: Holding the Dragon Egg grants passive powers.

- Keep inventory will be OFF inside the dimension.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Once the Ender Dragon is defeated for the first time, End Portals revert to normal behavior.
