
# The Nether

The Nether has been twicked to make it more interesting than vanilla.

---

### Environmental Hazards
- **Basalt Deltas**: Entering without a **Carved Pumpkin** causes blindness and slowness due to ash.

- **Soul Sand/Soil**: Walking on these blocks applies the **Wither** effect

- **Warped Nylium**: Stepping on it gives you a **levitation** effect.
