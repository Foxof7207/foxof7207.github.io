# 🌍 World Mechanics

Gilly-SMP features unique rules for teleportation, survival, and dimension traversal.

---

## Teleportation

Getting around the world quickly.

| Command | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| `/tpa <player>` | Send a teleport request to another player. |
| `/back` | Teleport back to your previous location. |
| `/suicide` | Instantly kill your character (use as a last resort). |

---

## Lifesteal

Steal hearts from your enemies and become the ultimate survivor!

### Mechanics
- **Lifesteal**: Gain a heart when you kill a player.
- **Elimination**: If you lose all your hearts, you are eliminated until a teammate revives you.
- **Withdrawal**: Turn extra hearts into physical items to trade or store.
- **Revival**: Use a **Revive Crystal** to bring back eliminated teammates.

| Command | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| `/hearts` | Check your current heart count. |
| `/withdrawheart <amount>` | Withdraw hearts into items. |
| `/revive <player>` | Revive an eliminated player. |
| `/lifestealz` | Open the main Lifesteal menu. |

---

## The Nether

Survive the harsh environments and unique phenomena of the Nether.

### Environmental Hazards
- **Basalt Deltas**: Entering without a **Carved Pumpkin** causes blindness and slowness due to ash.
- **Soul Sand/Soil**: Walking on these blocks applies the **Wither** effect.
- **Warped Nylium**: Stepping on it gives you a **levitation** effect.

---

## The END

Fight the Ender Dragon with special mechanics and challenges!

### Dragon Fight Features
- **Cooperative Fight**: Each player contributes up to 2 Eyes of Ender. All contributors must be present to activate the portal.
- **Harder Dragon**: The Ender Dragon has **1000 HP** and gains resistance as it takes damage.
- **Dragon Egg Buffs**: Holding the Dragon Egg grants passive powers.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Once the Ender Dragon is defeated for the first time, End Portals revert to normal behavior.
