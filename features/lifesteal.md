# Lifesteal

Steal hearts from your enemies and become the ultimate survivor!

### Mechanics
- **Lifesteal**: Gain a heart when you kill a player.
- **Elimination**: If you lose all your hearts, you are eliminated until a teammate revives you.
- **Withdrawal**: Turn extra hearts into physical items to trade or store.
- **Revival**: Use a **Revive Crystal** to bring back eliminated teammates.

| Command | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| `/hearts` | Check your current heart count. |
| `/withdrawheart <amount>` | Withdraw hearts into items. |
| `/revive <player>` | Revive an eliminated player. |
| `/lifestealz` | Open the main Lifesteal menu. |
