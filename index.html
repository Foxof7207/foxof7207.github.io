Server Commands

  • /f create - Create a new faction
  • /f join - Join a faction
  • /f leave - Leave your current faction
  • /f claim add - Claim land for your faction
  • /f unclaim - Unclaim land from your faction
  • /f map - View the faction map
  • /f home - Teleport to your faction's home
  • /f home set - Set your faction's home
  • /f invite add - Invite a player to your faction
  • /f kick - Kick a player from your faction
  • /f safe - Open your faction chest
  • /f rank promote - Promote a player in your faction
  • /f rank demote - Demote a player in your faction
  • /f modify description - Set your faction's description
  • /f modify motd - Set your faction's message of the day
  • /f modify open - Open your faction to public joining
  • /f modify close - Close your faction to public joining
  • /f declare ally - Ally with another faction
  • /f declare neutral - Set neutral relations with a faction
  • /f declare enemy - Set enemy relations with a faction
  • /f chat - Toggle faction chat
  • /tpa - Teleport you to someone else
  • /lastdeath - Teleport you where you died
  • /sethome - Set your home (You can have up to 2 homes)
  • /home - Teleport you to the specified home
  • /delhome - Delete a home
  • /homes - List your homes
  • /pvp - Turn ON or OFF your PVP
