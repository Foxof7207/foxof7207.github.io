Server Commands

  • Pickup: Shift + Right Click to pick up villagers
  • Place: Place villager head to release them (works in boats, minecarts)
  • Anchor: Shift + Right Click with Shears to prevent movement
  • Claim: Shift + Right Click with Shovel to protect from damage
  • Menu: Shift + Right Click claimed villager to change biome, trading, and partners
  • Hoppers: Craft with 1 Hopper + 1 Emerald to auto-pickup villagers
  • Trades: View trades in villager description, reset in trading menu
  • /claim [] - Claim a territory (chunk), with an option to set the radius
  • /claim main - Open the main gui of a claim
  • /claim settings [] - Open the settings gui of a claim
  • /claim members [] - Open the members gui of a claim
  • /claim chunks [] - Open the chunks gui of a claim
  • /claim add [<*|claim-name>] - Add a player to your claim, use '*' for all your claims
  • /claim remove [<*|claim-name>] - Remove a player from your claim, use '*' for all your claims
  • /claim ban [<*|claim-name>] - Ban a player from your claim, use '*' for all claims
  • /claim unban [<*|claim-name>] - Unban a player from your claim, use '*' for all your claims
  • /claim bans [] - Open the bans gui of a claim
  • /claim kick [<*|claim-name>] - Kick physically a player from your claim, use '*' for all your claims
  • /claim owner [<*|claim-name>] - Transfer the ownership of a claim, use '*' for all your territories
  • /claim tp - Teleport to one of your territory
  • /claim see [] - See the chunk you are in with border of particles
  • /claim map - Check the claims around you in a chat map
  • /claim automap - Check automatically the map when you change chunk
  • /claim autoclaim - Claim automatically the chunk when you change chunk
  • /claim autounclaim - Unclaim automatically the chunk when you change chunk
  • /claim fly - Enable/disable the claim fly
  • /claim autofly - Enable/disable the claim fly automatically
  • /claim list - Open the gui with your claims
  • /claim chat - Change your claim chat mode (public or claim chat)
  • /claim setspawn - Change the location of the claim's spawn
  • /claim setname - Change the name of one of your claims
  • /claim setdesc - Change the description of one of your claims
  • /claim merge - Merge two claims into one
  • /claim addchunk - Add the chunk you're standing at to a claim
  • /claim delchunk - Remove a chunk from a claim. Use format 'world;x;z'
  • /claim autoaddchunk - Add automatically the chunk to the specified claim
  • /claim autodelchunk - Remove automatically the chunk from the specified claim
  • /claims - Open the gui with all the claims of the server
  • /unclaim [] - Delete a claim, use '*' for all your claims
  • /tpa - Teleport you to someone else
  • /back - Teleport you back where you last where
