# Server Rules

## 1. Respect and Kindness
⟶ Treat all players with respect.  
⟶ Discriminatory, abusive language, or ping abuse is not tolerated.

## 2. No Cheating or Exploiting
⟶ Cheating, exploiting, or using third-party mods such as x-ray for an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden.  
⟶ Immediate disciplinary action will be taken against cheaters.

## 3. Claims rules
⟶ Do not intentionally claims natural structures. (Forteress, Trials Chambers, . . . )  
⟶ Merge your claim together as much as possible.  
⟶ Do not overclaim, only claim what you need.

## 4. Keep It Clean
⟶ No explicit, inappropriate, or NSFW content.  
⟶ Do not promote other server IPs.

## 5. Forbidden Clients
⟶ Meteor Client and all hacked clients are strictly forbidden.

## 6. No Griefing, Stealing, or Spawn Killing

## 7. Voice Chat rules
⟶ Speak clearly and avoid background noise.  
⟶ Use push-to-talk whenever possible.

## 8. Reporting Issues
⟶ Report rule violations (theft, spawn kills, griefing) to the Staff.  
⟶ Do not take matters into your own hands, we have tool to restitue the objects.

## 9. Enforcement
⟶ Admins reserve the right to modify, suspend, or enforce rules.  
⟶ Repeated violations may result in mutes, kicks, or account locks.
